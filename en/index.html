Do I need special equipment?
No. Fullsite runs on any computer or tablet with a browser. No local servers, no proprietary terminals. Use the hardware you already have.
¿Works offline?
Yes. Every terminal keeps taking orders, sending to kitchen, and processing payments even if the connection drops. When it returns, everything syncs automatically.

Yes. We already migrated a complete restaurant from Wansoft: 500+ dishes, 178 recipes with ingredients, 200 suppliers, and nearly three years of sales history.
